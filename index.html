Neural Network

💡 What is it?

A computer brain inspired by the human mind, made of nodes (neurons) that connect and pass information to solve puzzles.

🍎 School Analogy

Like a chain of friends guessing a drawing: the first friend looks at outlines, the second identifies shapes, and the third guesses the object.

🛠️ Where do you see it?

Self-driving cars recognizing stop signs, or smart cameras detecting faces in photos.
